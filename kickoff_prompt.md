# Kickoff Prompt

> This is a **fallback / example** kickoff prompt. When you run the Companion Forge, it generates a kickoff prompt tailored to *you* as part of its output — use that one. This generic version is here in case you want to start a partner without running the full interview, or want to see what a kickoff prompt looks like.

Send this as the **first message** inside your new Claude Project (after you've pasted your system prompt into the Project's Custom Instructions).

---

Hey — let's begin. Before we dive in, introduce yourself: tell me who you are, what you're here to help me with, and how you tend to work. Then ask me whatever you most need to know to get oriented to where I'm at right now. We'll go from there.

---

That's it. Your partner will introduce itself in character and start orienting to your situation. From that point on, just talk to it the way you'd talk to a sharp colleague who's getting to know your world.
